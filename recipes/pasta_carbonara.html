Pasta Carbonara

Description

Authentic pasta carbonara relies on just five core ingredients and a specific technique to create a creamy sauce without using any actual cream.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cured Pork
  • Cheese
  • Black Peppe

Preparation Steps

  1. Render the Pork
  2. Boil the Pasta
  3. Mix the Sauce Base
  4. Emulsify
  5. Toss Vigorously
